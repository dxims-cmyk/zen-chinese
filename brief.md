# ZEN CHINESE TAKEAWAY - MOCKUP SITE BUILD

You are building a mockup website for Zen Chinese Takeaway, a Chinese takeaway at 618 Alexandra Parade, Dennistoun, Glasgow G31 3BT. This is a Track 1 cold call build for AM:PM Media.

## About the Business
- Chinese takeaway on Alexandra Parade, Dennistoun (same street as Margherita Pizzeria and Celinos)
- Takeaway and delivery only, no dine-in
- Open 1pm to 11pm, 7 days a week
- Phone: 0141 556 1100
- Known locally for fresh, made-to-order food. Customers can see chefs cooking through the open kitchen
- Rice included in the price of main meals, generous portions, competitive pricing
- Popular dishes: chicken curry, chow mein, chop suey, lemon chicken, salt and chilli range, crispy duck
- No website exists. Only on Yelp and delivery apps
- Competitors: Lee Wah, Superwok, Sichuan House, Poa San (all Dennistoun/East End area)

## What the Site Needs to Communicate
- Fresh food, cooked to order right in front of you (open kitchen is a USP)
- Great value: rice included with every main, large portions
- Local Dennistoun favourite, not a chain
- Easy ordering: phone for pickup, links to delivery apps

## Design Direction
- Clean, modern, slightly bold. NOT the typical red and gold Chinese takeaway look
- Colour palette: Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a), rich red-orange (#D63031), warm off-white (#F9F6F2), subtle gold accent (#C7A951)
- The red should be used sparingly as an accent, not as a primary background
- Think clean Japanese-influenced minimalism applied to a Chinese takeaway
- Typography: A clean geometric sans for headings (e.g., Space Grotesk or DM Sans bold) + Inter or DM Sans for body
- The design should feel fresh and modern, setting it apart from every other Chinese takeaway in Glasgow

## Sections (single page, scroll)
1. **Hero** - Clean, bold. Business name "ZEN" large and typographic. Tagline: "Fresh. Fast. Made in front of you." Location and hours. Click-to-call button.
2. **What Makes Us Different** - 3 cards or points: "Cooked Fresh to Order" (open kitchen, watch your food being made), "Rice Included" (every main comes with rice, no hidden extras), "Dennistoun's Favourite" (local love, generous portions). Keep it punchy.
3. **Menu Highlights** - NOT a full menu. 6-8 most popular dishes styled as a grid. Salt and chilli chicken, crispy duck, chicken curry, sweet and sour, chow mein, special fried rice, etc. Use placeholder names until confirmed. Each with a short appetising description.
4. **Order** - Three options: Call Us (click-to-call, most prominent), Uber Eats, Just Eat. Direct ordering saves them money.
5. **Find Us** - Google Maps embed for 618 Alexandra Parade. Hours. Mention "Alexandra Parade, opposite [landmark if known]".
6. **Footer** - Phone, address. "Built by AM:PM Media" link.

## Technical Requirements
- Next.js 14 App Router
- Tailwind CSS
- Google Fonts (Space Grotesk + DM Sans, or DM Sans for both)
- Mobile-first responsive
- Click-to-call on all phone numbers
- Schema.org LocalBusiness JSON-LD
- No em dashes in any copy
- All images use next/image with placeholder blur

## Copy Rules
- No em dashes
- No AI language (elevate, streamline, curate, innovative)
- Short, punchy. This is a takeaway, not a fine dining restaurant. Keep it casual and direct.
- The tone should feel like a mate recommending their go-to Chinese

## Token Efficiency
- NO sub-agents. Build sequentially.
- Don't re-read files you've already seen.
- Keep responses short during builds.
- Install deps first, then build file by file.
